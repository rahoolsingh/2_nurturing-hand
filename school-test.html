Welcome to Indus Oak

The Indus Oak Playschool, an initiative by Nurturing Hand. Our playschool is a safe and nurturing environment where your child will learn and grow through play-based learning. Our dedicated teachers and staff ensure that your child receives the best care and attention possible, while our carefully designed curriculum encourages exploration, creativity, and critical thinking.

At The Indus Oak Playschool, we believe that every child is unique and has their own way of learning. Our curriculum is designed to be inclusive and cater to the individual needs of each child, helping them develop their skills and talents at their own pace. We provide a stimulating and fun-filled environment that encourages children to learn through play and exploration.

We understand that as parents, you want the best for your child. That's why we have a team of experienced and caring teachers who work tirelessly to ensure that your child receives the best education possible. Our school is equipped with modern facilities and resources, ensuring that your child's learning journey is fun, engaging, and effective.

At The Indus Oak Playschool, we value diversity, creativity, and community. We welcome children from all backgrounds and cultures, and our aim is to instill in them a love for learning, a sense of curiosity, and a strong foundation for a lifetime of learning.

Join us at The Indus Oak Playschool and give your child the gift of learning, growth, and discovery.
