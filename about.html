Welcome To Nurturing Hand

NEW VISION, GREAT MISSION

Nurturing Hand is a non-governmental organization (NGO) that was founded by Mrs. Seema Singh. Our mission is to empower women, educate children, and protect the environment by reutilizing waste for decoration purposes.

Our primary goal is to create awareness about the importance of recycling and upcycling in our daily lives. By doing so, we aim to reduce the amount of waste that is generated and protect our planet though our "JunkArt" initiative.

We are also committed to providing education to children through our "Indus Oak Playschool". Our focus is on providing quality education to children to help them develop the skills they need to succeed in life.

At Nurturing Hand, we believe that "women empowerment" is key to the progress of society. We strive to create opportunities for women to learn new skills and become financially independent. Through our various initiatives, we aim to help women achieve their full potential and contribute to their families and communities.

We are dedicated to making a positive impact on the world around us and are grateful for your support in helping us achieve our goals.

Together, we can make a lasting impact on our world.

At Nurturing Hand, our mission is to empower women, educate communities, and protect the environment.
